DAY 1: KELOR & KALONG

Trekking on Kelor Island for panoramic views, followed by snorkeling in Manjarite. Sunset cruise at Kalong Island to see thousands of flying foxes.

2

Snorkeling with Manta Rays at Manta Point and Taka Makassar. Return to Labuan Bajo harbor by late afternoon.
